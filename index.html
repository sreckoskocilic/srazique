    How to Play

    Objective

    Capture opponent pegs and be the last player remaining on the board!

    Gameplay

    • Select a peg — Click or use arrow keys to navigate, Enter to select
    • Choose a tile — Move to an adjacent tile to answer a question
    • Answer correctly — Your peg advances in rank (Kmet → Vojnik → Vitez)
    • Answer incorrectly — Your peg may be pushed back or captured!

    Combat

    • Moving onto an opponent's tile triggers COMBAT
    • Answer two questions to determine the victor
    • Winner stays, loser is eliminated

    Flag Tiles

    Corner tiles have flags — capture them for bonus points!

    Keyboard Controls

    • Arrow keys — Navigate board
    • Enter/Space — Select peg or tile
    • A/B/C/D or 1/2/3/4 — Select answer
    • Escape — Cancel/pause
